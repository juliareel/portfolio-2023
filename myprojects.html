Euchre Simulator

×

Wrote a program in C++ to simulate the card game Euchre, creating a player algorithm that competes against human players. Gained experience using abstract data types, object-oriented programming, and polymorphism. Euchre is a trick-taking card game popular in Michigan. It is most commonly played by four people in two partnerships with a deck of 24 cards. Partnerships accumulate points for winning tricks, and the game continues until one side reaches the maximum number of points. Implemented a "simple" computer-controlled player strategy following a pre-defined set of rules and strategies.
